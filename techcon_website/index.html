Welcome to TechCon 2024

TechCon 2024 is a global technology conference bringing together innovators, developers, and industry leaders to explore the future of tech. Join us for keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities.

Brief History of TechCon 2024

TechCon 2024 was the flagship technical conference organized by the Semiconductor Research Corporation (SRC), held from September 8–10, 2024, at the Renaissance Austin Hotel in Austin, Texas.
This annual event serves as a premier platform for showcasing cutting-edge research in semiconductor electronics, bringing together top researchers, scientists, and students from across the globe.

Mission

The primary mission of TechCon is to facilitate the exchange of innovative ideas, foster collaboration among
industry leaders and academic researchers, and celebrate advancements in semiconductor technology.
The conference aims to:

  • Highlight groundbreaking research and technological developments.
  • Provide networking opportunities for students and professionals.
  • Promote diversity and inclusion within the tech community.
  • Support career development through recruiting events.

In 2024, the conference featured over 200 invited SRC Research Scholars, including nearly 50 undergraduate
researchers, and saw participation from attendees across the United States and internationally.
